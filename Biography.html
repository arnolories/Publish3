So...About me.

Hi, the name's Arno and i've been drawing things for a while now. Ever since I can remeber actually.. Since i was but a wee lad I would spend hours drawing my favourite cartoon-characters, always in black & white though.. i'm a simple guy, I like simple tools. So most of the stuff you'll find here are pen-drawings and some ink-paintings. Usually a little dark (but hey, who isn't these days right?) but I try to sneak a little humour in there every now and then. Hell, look at this kid next to the words you're reading right now, hilarious stuff!  Anyways, I'm currently studying at an Art College in Ghent (Illustration, go figure.), no indie-selfmade-artist path for me, no sir! I've crumbled under the man! So a lot of the stuff you'll see is also made for my studies. Have a look around, if you find some stuf you like: send me a message.
Or don't. It's your life man, have fun.
